About Us!

Olive Garden is an American dining restaurant that specializes in Italian-American cuisine. Founded in 1982, Olive Garden takes pride in its food as well as its customers. We try our best to please you through our cuisine, as well as our service. Without you all, Olive Garden wouldn’t be what it is today.
